# ELUXR v3 Progress Report — March 20, 2026

## Session Summary

Major overhaul of product scraping pipeline, frontend UX improvements, and n8n workflow enhancements.

---

## Changes Made

### Product Scraping Pipeline (n8n Workflow 01)

- **Multi-page scraping**: Added pagination support — scrapes 3 pages of product listings (Find Products Page → Scrape Shop Page 1/2/3) for sites with paginated catalogs like heinzmayer.de
- **Perplexity enrichment**: Always runs after initial scraping to find specific product URLs (replaces generic collection URLs with individual product page URLs)
- **Perplexity fallback**: When scraping yields 0 products (JS-heavy SPAs like tiffany.com), Perplexity researches the brand catalog and Claude structures results into products
- **Perplexity image search**: After saving products, searches for product images from press sites and retail aggregators
- **Fixed Perplexity auth**: Research Products node now uses n8n credential store instead of hardcoded invalid API key
- **Fixed `fetch is not defined`**: Update Product Images Bulk node uses `$http.request` instead of `fetch`
- **Fixed webhook error handling**: Added `onError: continueRegularOutput` to webhook node
- **Improved Claude extraction prompt**: Extracts products from nav menus, collection links, and category pages — not just product detail cards

### Content Date Scheduling (n8n Workflow 04)

- **Dates start from today**: Changed `Prepare Daily Content Loop` to calculate `scheduled_date` from current date instead of day 1 of the month. Week 1 Day 1 = today, Day 2 = tomorrow, etc.

### Image Generation (n8n Social Calendar Image Generator)

- **Aspect ratio**: Changed from 9:16 to 4:5 (Instagram vertical)
- **Product type awareness**: Frontend now sends `product_name` and `product_category` to the image gen webhook
- **Prompt engineer updated**: Strict rules for body placement — bracelets on wrists, necklaces on necks, earrings on ears, rings on fingers. Product name/category overrides image analysis

### Frontend (index.html)

#### Step 1
- Storytelling themes are now collapsible/expandable dropdowns with day-by-day breakdown (Mon-Sun theme + tagline)

#### Step 2
- Removed hard 28-product cap — shows all scraped products
- Users can proceed to Step 3 with 7+ products (was 28)
- Counter shows just the count (was "X / 28")

#### Step 3
- Product images clickable to open product page in new tab
- Product name shown below image
- After content generation completes, topics grid stays visible (no auto-scroll to Step 5)
- Fixed `businessContext` null crash — added `getBusinessContext()` fallback for when page is refreshed

#### Step 4
- Product image + name displayed on right side of day modal topic header, clickable to product page
- Loading spinner inside day modal when image is generating
- Loading spinner on day cards in the grid during image generation
- Download buttons for generated images and videos

#### Step 5
- Rewrote `loadScheduleThemes` — uses `contentData` directly, no more fragile calData chain
- Schedule starts from today (not calendar week start)
- Prev/Next week buttons work without resetting state
- Day cards show approved image thumbnails, platform dots (IG/FB/YT/TT/LI/X), post previews
- Clicking day card shows full content with images, videos, platform posts, approve/reject buttons
- Fixed `formatDateString` timezone bug (local date instead of UTC)
- Fixed `allItems` undefined ReferenceError that caused blank schedule
