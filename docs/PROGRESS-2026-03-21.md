## Current Pipeline Flow (end-to-end)

1. **Step 1** → User enters URL, industry, platforms, storytelling theme → "Analyze My Business"
2. **Pipeline Orchestrator** (WF 14) → triggers WF 01
3. **WF 01** → Jina scrapes homepage + 3 product pages → Claude extracts products → Perplexity enriches URLs → Perplexity searches images → saves products + ICP to Supabase
4. **Step 2** → Products displayed with images, URLs, drag-to-reorder
5. **Step 3** → "Generate Topics" sends 7 ordered products → WF 15 generates topics with explicit product-day assignments → topics displayed with product images
6. **Step 3** → "Approve All & Generate Content" → WF 04 generates platform posts starting from today's date
7. **Step 4** → Day modal shows posts, image gen with spinner, download buttons
8. **Step 5** → Schedule from today, prev/next navigation, day cards with content
